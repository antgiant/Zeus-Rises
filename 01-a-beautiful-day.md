# Chapter 1 – A Beautiful Day

It is such a beautiful day thought Jane as she made her way across campus to her next class. When she made it to the campus quad Jane found herself almost involuntarily stepping out of the hurried crush to take in the view. 

The green expanse was dotted with students lounging in the sun here or playing catch there. Crazy Joe was over in his usual corner preaching the end of the world or something, and there were even a few student groups who hadn’t give up recruiting yet. With the unexpected and very welcome cooler temperatures a few leaves had even gotten an early jump on changing colors. 

But the sky! 

The sky was just amazing! A perfectly clear, crystalline blue as far as the eye could see. It was almost beautiful enough to make up for the sun blotting out all the other celestial objects thought Jane. Well almost all of them, Jane smiled to herself as she picked out the pinprick of Venus. Just then the toll of the bells reminded Jane that she was about be late for class. So, she reluctantly rejoined the crush and headed across the quad to Copernicus hall. As she made her way across the quad, she found herself picking up snatches of conversations.

“Did you hear about what happened last night after …”

“What did you get for problem 15?”

“Here’s an easy catch butterfingers!”

“No mom, of course I’m not skipping class, I’m just…”

“BUT THEN THE FLOOD KILLED THEM ALL!  JUST LIKE THE COMING FLAMES OF HELL WILL…”

“WATCH OUT! Frisbee!”

“Yes, but as Plato’s cave makes clear”

“Drama club always needs more glitter!”

Smiling to herself at the thought of Plato’s cave filled with glittery drama club members Jane walked into Copernicus Hall. Her smile quickly melted quickly into resolve as she looked down the hallway and saw John standing at the door of 105 staring right at her with a triumphant smile of recognition.
